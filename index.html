PokéPlaylist

Welcome to PokéPlaylist — the site that turns your dream Pokémon team into a reccommended playlist!

To get started, you can search for Pokémon by name or pokédex number. If you want to add a Pokémon to your team, just drag and drop into Your Team in the sidebar. Click View Pokémon team to open the sidebar. When you're happy with the 6 you've selected, click Generate PokéPlaylist to see the reccommended songs based on your selection. Your team will be saved for future reference, but you can start over anytime.
