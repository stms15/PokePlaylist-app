PokéPlaylist

Welcome to PokéPlaylist — the site that turns your dream Pokémon team into a playlist reccommended by Spotify!

To get started, search for Pokémon in the first generation by name or filter by type (e.g. water, fire, etc.). If you want to add a Pokémon to your team, just click, drag, and drop into “Your Team” in the sidebar. When you're happy with the 6 you've selected, click “Generate Playlist” to see the reccommended songs based on your selection. Your team and playlist will be saved for future reference, but if you wish to start over, just click “Pick a new team.”

Have fun and choose wisely...
