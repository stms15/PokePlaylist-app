PokéPlaylist

Welcome to PokéPlaylist — the site that turns your dream Pokémon team into a reccommended playlist!

To get started, you can search for Pokémon in the first generation by name or filter by type. If you want to add a Pokémon to your team, just drag and drop into Your Team in the sidebar. When you're happy with the 6 you've selected, click Generate Playlist to see the reccommended songs based on your selection. Your team will be saved for future reference, but you can start over anytime.

Have fun! And choose wisely...
